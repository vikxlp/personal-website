I've been missing the amazing coffee I had in Chiang Mai & Bangkok last year. It's quite hot in Bangalore and I've discovered a new spot to get my favourite coffee. [TEKA](https://maps.app.goo.gl/Wx8yVHJ42UhxD7sS8) - a matcha cafe run by a Japanese lady in the Indira Nagar.

I like my coffee cold and black. I usually go for iced pourover if the beans have interesting note. Second, it's coffee + soda + citrus fruit. The freshness of an orange or a yuzu with boost of caffiene and soda is a great combination. I like vanilla as a flavour as well.

And here's more of what I'm enjoying recently.

Listening - [Hoggar by Tinariwen](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL70ZCHisQRC1kXY_qmNkkgvJaC3MFvLvr)   
New album with amazing music and visual art. Tinariwen is a desert blues band from Sahara, has an interesting origin story.

Reading - [The Last Quiet Thing by Terry Godier](https://www.terrygodier.com/the-last-quiet-thing)  
Terry Godier is a designer writing about user's relationship with apps nad device. Here he writes about how we are surrounded by things which constantly need our attention.

Watching - [Jet Lag: The Game](https://www.youtube.com/@jetlagthegame)  
Discovered this series last year, and Bhavya & I got hooked. It combines travel, game mechanics, lovely landscapes & public infrastructure. What more one can ask!
